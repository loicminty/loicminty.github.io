bio

Loic Minty, also known as Sleep Cell, is a sound artist, digital instrument maker, and cultural mediator. His works reflect on the accelerating pace of the modern world, as well as exploring the emotional qualities of electronic music. Inspired by Pauline Oliveros's Deep Listening and the american avant-garde, he launched the Inner-Space event series to showcase spiritually-guided practices, and promote collective listening.

Enhancing listening is also part of his work as a music journalist, where he stands out for his academic background in the sociology of science and the history of electroacoustics. From a McLuhanian approach, Minty passes through music to take on the modern problems of media, consumption, and sensory saturation, tracing how digital infrastructures rewire attention and reshape cultural presence.
